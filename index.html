Gym Room in Hong Kong

This map shows where to find the a gym to work out in Hong Kong. The data were stored by government in json format. It required me to convert the Degree/Minutes/Seconds (DMS) back to Decimal Degrees (DD) via SQL in order to let ArcGIS Pro be able to read it as geojson data

This idea is based on the #30DayMapChallenge, a daily mapping challenge open to everyone and the category for this map is "Points"

Skills: ArcGIS, SQL, json, coordination system understanding

Hong Kong's Street Density

This map presents the traffic load in Hong Kong in the shape of hexagon. To achieve, tessellation was generate based on the shape of AOI. The hexagon shapefile was then spatial joint with the Hong Kong street data to identify how crowd the road are. This idea is based on the #30DayMapChallenge, a daily mapping challenge open to everyone and the category for this map is "Hexagons"

Skills: ArcGIS Pro, Generate Tessellation, Spatial Joint
